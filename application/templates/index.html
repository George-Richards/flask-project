Welcome to the recent form app!


This app allows you to keep a record of
premier league games. When you add an
entry the game will be added to that
teams relevant database. You can add in
a comment to each of the games such as
"captain injured" or "chelsea red card"
and from there can choose whether or not
to include that game in your recent form.
